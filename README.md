# Supplio Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Overview

Supplio is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

## Data Storage

All data — including your meal plans, preferences, cooking history, favorites, and custom meals — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. Your data is never uploaded to any cloud service.

## Health Data

Supplio collects body metrics (height, weight, age, biological sex, and activity level) solely to calculate your recommended daily calorie intake using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation. These metrics are stored exclusively on your device, are never transmitted to any server, and are never shared with third parties. You can delete this data at any time by deleting the app.

## AI Features

Supplio uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models for AI-powered features like cooking tips and the cooking assistant. For premium subscribers who enable the cloud AI option, queries are sent to OpenAI's API (servers located in the United States) for processing. These queries may include meal names, ingredient lists, dietary preferences (including allergen information), and your cooking questions. You can disable cloud AI at any time in Settings.

**Data Transfer & Retention:** When cloud AI is used, data is transferred to OpenAI under OpenAI's Data Processing Addendum (DPA). OpenAI's API does not use submitted prompts to train models by default and retains API data for up to 30 days for safety and abuse monitoring purposes, after which it is deleted. See OpenAI's privacy policy at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy for full details. The legal basis for this transfer is your explicit consent (given when enabling cloud AI) and the performance of the service you requested.

## Analytics

Supplio does not collect analytics, usage data, or crash reports. We have no tracking, no third-party SDKs, and no advertising frameworks.

## Subscriptions

If you subscribe to Supplio Premium, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

## Third-Party Services

When cloud AI is enabled, Supplio sends cooking-related queries to OpenAI's API. No other third-party services are integrated. There are no social logins, no advertising SDKs, and no data sharing beyond the optional cloud AI feature.

## Health & Allergen Information

Supplio provides general nutritional estimates and automated allergen detection for informational purposes only. This information is not medical advice. Allergen detection uses keyword matching and may not identify all allergens, particularly in compound ingredients or sauces. Always verify ingredients independently if you have a food allergy. Consult a healthcare professional for dietary advice.

## Children's Privacy

Supplio is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that data. Under COPPA (US), GDPR (EU), and equivalent Swiss law, we require users to be at least 13 years of age before using the health data features of this app.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app. No account creation is required.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at feedback@supplio.ch.
