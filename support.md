## Common Questions
      
**How do I reset my meal plan?**
Go to the Meal Plan tab and generate a new plan.
      
**How do I change my calorie goal?**
Go to Settings and update your profile information.
      
**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Supplio > Cancel.
      
**Where is my data stored?**
All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
