      11 ## Common Questions
      12
      13 **How do I reset my meal plan?**
      14 Go to the Meal Plan tab and generate a new plan.
      15
      16 **How do I change my calorie goal?**
      17 Go to Settings and update your profile information.
      18
      19 **How do I cancel my subscription?**
      20 Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Supplio > Cancel.
      21
      22 **Where is my data stored?**
      23 All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
